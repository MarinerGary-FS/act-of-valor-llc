# Interaction Behavior — Act of Valor

## Core Objective
Guide users to take action without hesitation.

The site must feel:
- Responsive
- Reassuring
- Alive (but not distracting)

---

## HOVER INTERACTIONS

### Service Cards
- Slight upward lift on hover
- Soft shadow increase
- Subtle highlight border

Purpose:
Encourage engagement without distraction

---

## CTA BUTTONS

### Behavior:
- Slight scale increase on hover
- Color shift (subtle, not aggressive)

### Priority:
Primary CTA (Call Now) must stand out at all times

---

## SCROLL BEHAVIOR

- Smooth scrolling between sections
- Sections fade in subtly (no heavy animation)

---

## STICKY HEADER

- Always visible
- Phone number clickable
- CTA button present

---

## MOBILE INTERACTION

- Large tap targets
- One-thumb usability
- No clutter

---

## FORM INTERACTION

- Instant feedback on submit
- Minimal fields
- Clear success message

---

## CHAT / ASSISTANT BEHAVIOR

- Appears after short delay
- Offers help (not aggressive)

Example:
"Need help right now? We’re here."

---

## TRUST INTERACTION

- Credentials section:
  - Hover reveals slight emphasis
  - Optional tooltip for details

---

## USER FLOW PRIORITY

1. Land on page
2. Identify problem
3. Click CTA or call
4. Submit or connect

Every interaction must support this flow
